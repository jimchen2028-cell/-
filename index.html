This model is intentionally simple and focuses only on three physical attributes. It doesn’t capture technical ability, decision making, or tactical intelligence – all the things that make real pros special.
How the score works
  • Your score is a weighted mix: 40% speed, 30% strength, 30% stamina.
  • Each slider is on a 0–100 scale; you can later tie these to test data (e.g. 30m times, Yo-Yo test levels).
  • The goal isn’t to “prove who’s better” but to give structure to debates and show where your physical profile leans.
